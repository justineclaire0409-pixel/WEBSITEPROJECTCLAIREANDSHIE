Our Boutique Story

Veloura Serélyn began as a quiet dream under the golden sun of the Philippines. Founded in 2026, our journey started with a simple belief: that elegance should be effortless, and every person is a living masterpiece waiting to be adorned.

The name Veloura evokes the soft touch of velvet, while Serélyn reflects a serene, radiant spirit. We curate more than just products; we curate moments of magic. From the shimmer of a necklace to the silhouette of a dreamy dress, each piece is chosen to make your heart flutter.

We are a sanctuary for the soft-hearted and the bold-souled. Our mission is to celebrate your unique beauty, giving you the confidence to radiate luxury in your everyday life.

Thank you for being part of our unfolding story. May your style always be as luminous as your spirit.
